# Summary Data from the accelerometers from the Samsung Galaxy S Smartphone

###Activity 
character
>indicates the type of activity performed for the measure:
> - walking
> - walking upstairs
> - walking downstairs
> - sitting
> - standing
> - laying

###Subject.Id
integer
>indicates the subject for the measure
